    • What is Yoga for me ?

    For me, yoga has always been much more about the emotional than the physical. The tension we hold in our hearts is reflected in the body. Asana is a way to see what needs more love and healing on the inside. This is why I practice: everything I do on the mat is valuable. Even in the difficult moments — especially in the difficult moments — I'm on a journey that opens my heart. And sometimes my heart cracks open just enough for my body to open up, too.
